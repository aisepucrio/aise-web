SMP (BuddyMents) — Mental Health & Productivity for Teams

One-liner
A mobile-first app that helps companies track mental-health and productivity signals—without creepy surveillance—using short daily check-ins and team dashboards.

Why it exists
Spreadsheet surveys are clunky, versioned to chaos, and give leaders zero real-time signal. SMP replaces them with guided flows, a metrics model grounded in research, and audit-ready exports. Core metrics are inspired by AISE Lab’s study on mental-health/productivity measurements (e.g., Self Vision, Team Vision, Work Environment Conditions, Absenteeism, Motivation/QSI) and simplified for everyday use.

What the app does (feature sketch)

Onboarding & Auth: email/OTP, password reset, social sign-in.

Daily check-ins: 10-second sliders for Self Vision; optional peer rating for Team Vision; checklist for Work Environment Conditions; quick Absenteeism reason logging.

Dashboards: team trends, alignment gaps (self vs. peer), risk flags, and environment rankings.

Admin console: create teams, invite members, define cadences, export CSV/JSONL, manage privacy and data retention.

Metric snapshots (operationalized)

Self Vision (IPP): 1–10 self-rating of daily productivity.

Team Vision (TPP): average peer rating per person.

Work Vision: alignment score derived from IPP vs. TPP.

Work Environment Conditions: % of green checks on an ergonomic checklist (lighting, noise, air, equipment, etc.).

Absenteeism: rate of last-minute or unjustified absences.

Motivation & QSI: composite from social-interaction tone and work habits (kept lightweight for v1).
